# Banana Pudding

## Ingredients

* 8 **bananas**
* 2 packages **instant vanilla pudding mix**
* 1 can **Eagle brand milk**
* 4 cups of **milk**
* 1 box of **vanilla wafers**
* 1 16oz tub **Cool whip**

## Instructions

1. Mix Cool Whip, milk, Eagle brand milk, and dry pudding
2. Layer wafers, bananas and pudding. Repeat
